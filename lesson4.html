Leading Culture and Inclusivity Takeaways

Culture fills out the blank when rules and procedures are absent

Over this topic, we covered how powerful culture can be.
We discussed cultural differences during the class and experienced the problem with prior assumptions through the card game.

Takeaways from the gold watch case

  • Prior assumptions change the narrative
  • Every situation could be viewed differently depending on your background

Takeaways from the card game

  • The idea the minority should play by “house rules”
  • Approach organization more like rounds 3 and 4. Everybody might not value the same things
  • Sometimes the things we carry on culturally are outdated, and we don’t even know the origin of it
