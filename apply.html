How To Apply

When looking forward to my professional career, there are some key concepts I could apply. As a future Product Manager, there are some key lessons I will apply in the future.

  • Listening - after practicing listening, I realize how it can increase my effectiveness. It will allow me to get a better understanding of various problems and therefore find more precise solutions
  • If you're not upsetting people, you're not creating change - as a Product Manager, you have to be creative and innovative. Creativity and innovation create change, and as we learned, people aren't comfortable with change
  • Collaboration - to be a successful Product Manager, you have to get the perspective of many stakeholders. The ability to bring those ideas together and communicate them between the stakeholders will be key
  • Don't wait to be influential - my next job will be at a new company for the first time in a while. Taking the initiative and assuming a leadership rule without waiting to receive one is crucial
  • EQ doesn't mean being nice - I tend to avoid confrontation and be too nice. Understanding that to be an effective leader, I can't always be nice is a big takeaway
