Leading by Listening Takeaways

Over this topic, we covered the importance of effective listening.
During the class, we discussed how Rachel Weldon's listening abilities positioned her for success and practiced effective listening in pairs.

Takeaways from Rachel Weldon case

  • In high-anxiety situations, we are more inclined to follow advice, but our judgment of choosing good advice is tempered
  • Listening in advance made it easier to understand her employees
  • Listen, listen, and decide

Takeaways from listening excersie

  • Truth comes after the pause
  • Listening to another person so they can hear themselves
  • Letting people speak without interruption helps them to express themselves and tell more detailed and interesting stories
